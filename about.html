Our lead folder is a member of the British Origami Society , one of the largest Origami organisations around for over 50 years. We also host workshops as part of the active and popular SOAS Origami Society which has been going strong for over 50 years. Currently we don't charge for our workshops but ask our clients to provide us with materials and travel reimbursement (more detail provided upon contact).

Some of our past clients and projects include:

  • Bloomsbury festival 2018 Origami phonebox installation
  • SOAS Welcome week workshops
  • Team-building activity at London Zoo
  • Community workshop as part of Rumi's KITCHEN/CAVE
